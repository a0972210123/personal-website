短影音製作方法

Short-Form Video Production

從前 3 秒的 hook 到一源多用的內容系統——短影音先求量與迭代速度，再求精。

From the 3-second hook to a repurposing system — in short-form video, volume and iteration speed come before polish.

🎯 學完你能做到 🎯 Learning Goals ① 用四段式框架寫出三種不同 hook 的 30 秒腳本 ② 建立自己的剪輯工具鏈並產出符合三平台 safe zone 的成品 ③ 設計「一次錄製、四種產出」的內容系統與每週節奏表 ① Write 30s scripts with three different hooks using the 4-part framework ② Build your editing toolchain and produce safe-zone-compliant output ③ Design a "record once, publish four ways" system with a weekly cadence

  • ~71% 觀眾在前 3 秒決定留下或滑走——hook 是整支影片最重要的 3 秒
  • ~71% of viewers decide in the first 3 seconds — the hook is the most important part of the video
  • Retention 技巧：不留 dead air——每 3-5 秒一個視覺變化（jump cut、B-roll、zoom、字卡）；中段埋「等一下有反轉」的預告
  • Retention tactics: no dead air — a visual change every 3-5 seconds (jump cuts, B-roll, zooms, text cards); tease a twist mid-video
  • 先兌現再要求：payoff 先兌現 hook 的承諾，再給單一明確 CTA
  • Deliver before asking: pay off the hook's promise first, then give one clear CTA
  • 平台節奏差異：Reels 重 3 秒留存、7-15 秒表現好；TikTok 適合 13-60 秒
  • Platform rhythm: Reels favors 7-15s with heavy 3s retention; TikTok suits 13-60s
誤區：開頭放 logo 動畫或自我介紹（前 3 秒被浪費）；hook 過度承諾但兌現不了（完播率崩、被降權）；一支影片塞多個 CTA（觀眾一個都不做）。Pitfalls: opening with a logo or self-intro (wasting the first 3s); over-promising hooks that don't pay off (completion crashes); stacking multiple CTAs (viewers do none).

Six Hook Formulas

Your 3-second arsenal

▾

1. 反直覺陳述Contrarian Statement

「大家都教你 X，但 X 正是你失敗的原因」

"Everyone teaches X — and X is exactly why you're failing"

2. 驚人數據Shocking Statistic

「97% 的 Pinterest 搜尋不含品牌名」

"97% of Pinterest searches contain no brand name"

3. 大膽提問Bold Question

「你知道你的影片為什麼沒人看完嗎？」

"Do you know why nobody finishes your videos?"

4. 視覺 Pattern InterruptVisual Pattern Interrupt

開頭放最戲劇性的畫面（成品 reveal、意外瞬間）再倒敘

Open with the most dramatic frame (the reveal, the surprise), then rewind

5. 「別做 X，改做 Y」"Stop X, Do Y Instead"

「別再買現成的捕夢網了」

"Stop buying mass-produced dreamcatchers"

6. 開放式懸念Curiosity Gap

「這個圖案是一條數學公式畫出來的」

"This pattern was drawn by a single math formula"

💡 同一支影片可以剪三個版本、各用不同 hook 發布測試——hook 是最值得 A/B 的變數。

💡 Cut three versions of the same video with different hooks — the hook is the single most testable variable.

字幕、標題與封面
Captions, Headlines & Covers
大量觀眾靜音觀看——字幕不是選配
Many watch on mute — captions aren't optional
Week 9
▾
  • 一律上字幕；auto-caption 準確率 90-95%，中文務必人工校對（同音字錯誤傷專業感）
  • Always caption; auto-captions hit 90-95% accuracy — Chinese must be proofread (homophones)
  • 字幕設計：粗體 sans-serif、高對比、描邊/陰影、一次 1-2 行、karaoke 式逐字高亮是主流
  • Design: bold sans-serif, high contrast, stroke/shadow, 1-2 lines max, karaoke word-by-word highlight is the norm
  • On-screen headline：前 3 秒畫面中上放一句話重述 hook——同時是「靜音版 hook」與搜尋關鍵字訊號（social SEO）
  • On-screen headline: restate the hook top-center in the first 3s — it's both a "mute-mode hook" and a search keyword signal
  • 工具：CapCut Auto Caption（免費、中文可用）、Submagic、Descript；輸出前套 safe zone 模板
  • Tools: CapCut Auto Caption (free, Chinese-capable), Submagic, Descript; check safe zones before export
誤區：auto-caption 不校對直發；字幕蓋住畫面主體或落在 UI 遮擋區；花俏字型＋彩虹色＋過多動畫（可讀性下降）。Pitfalls: publishing unproofed auto-captions; captions covering the subject or UI zones; fancy fonts + rainbow colors + excessive animation.

發布策略：頻率、時段、Hashtag 現況
Publishing: Cadence, Timing, Hashtags
穩定 > 爆量 · Social SEO 已取代 hashtag
Consistency beats bursts · Social SEO has replaced hashtags
Week 102025-26 轉變
▾
  • 頻率共識：每週 3-5 支；穩定 > 爆量——「爆發連發後消失兩週」是最傷演算法的行為；每週 3 支好片勝過 7 支平庸片
  • Cadence consensus: 3-5/week; consistency beats bursts — "binge-post then vanish" is the worst pattern; 3 good videos beat 7 mediocre ones
  • 時段（起點參考，以自己後台為準）：TikTok 週二至五下午 1-6 點；Reels 週三、五；Shorts 週五傍晚——高峰前 1-2 小時發
  • Timing (starting points only): TikTok Tue-Fri 1-6pm; Reels Wed/Fri; Shorts Fri evening — post 1-2 hours before peak
  • Hashtag 2025-26：還有用但已非觸及主力——平台轉向 social SEO（caption 關鍵字、口說內容、螢幕文字才是索引主力）
  • Hashtags 2025-26: still useful, no longer the reach lever — platforms shifted to social SEO (caption keywords, spoken words, on-screen text)
  • 做法：3-5 個高相關 niche tag（利基＋地區），放棄 30 個泛用 tag（#fyp 類幾乎無效）
  • Practice: 3-5 relevant niche tags (niche + region); drop the 30 generic ones (#fyp is nearly useless)
  • Caption：TikTok 支援 4000 字，塞自然語句關鍵字利搜尋；Reels 傾向 100-300 字
  • Captions: TikTok allows 4000 chars — use natural-language keywords; Reels prefers 100-300 chars
誤區：迷信「完美發文時間」犧牲一致性；三平台發相同檔案不改 caption/關鍵字；caption 只寫一句 emoji（浪費 social SEO 版位）。Pitfalls: chasing "perfect timing" at the cost of consistency; identical files across platforms without caption changes; emoji-only captions (wasting social SEO real estate).

實作練習
Practice Exercises
三週做完，你會有一條自己的短影音生產線
Three weeks to your own short-form pipeline
動手做 Hands-on
▾
練習 1（W8）：三支不同 hook 的腳本 Exercise 1 (W8): Three Scripts, Three Hooks 為捕夢網寫 3 支 30 秒腳本，各用不同 hook 公式：①成品 reveal 倒敘（視覺 pattern interrupt）②「別再買現成的捕夢網」（別做 X 改做 Y）③「這個圖案是數學公式畫的」（懸念）。每支標注四段的秒數分配。 Write three 30s dreamcatcher scripts with different hooks: ① reveal-first rewind (pattern interrupt) ② "stop buying mass-produced" (Stop X Do Y) ③ "drawn by a math formula" (curiosity gap). Mark the 4-part timing on each.
練習 2（W9）：剪出一支成品 Exercise 2 (W9): Cut One Finished Video 用 CapCut 把練習 1 其中一支剪成成品：中文字幕（人工校對）＋前 3 秒 on-screen headline＋safe zone 檢查，輸出 1080×1920 母檔。 Edit one script into a finished video in CapCut: proofread Chinese captions + 3s on-screen headline + safe-zone check, exported at 1080×1920.
練習 3（W10）：每週內容節奏表 Exercise 3 (W10): Weekly Content Cadence 設計捕夢網的每週節奏：1 支主片 → 切 2 支變體（不同 hook）→ 1 組金句 carousel → caption 關鍵字清單（中英各版）。排進行事曆，執行兩週後回顧數據。 Design the weekly cadence: 1 main video → 2 hook variants → 1 quote carousel → caption keyword lists (zh/EN). Schedule it, run for two weeks, review the data.

📎 產業備註：手作類 process video 一次拍攝可切多支不同 hook（脫模瞬間/上色/成品轉盤）；教育類用 Descript 逐字稿工作流最順；SaaS 用 screen recording 每個 feature 一支 30 秒。

📎 Industry notes: handmade process videos yield multiple hooks per shoot; education flows best through Descript transcripts; SaaS — one 30s screen recording per feature.
