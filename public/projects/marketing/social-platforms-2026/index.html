  • SEO uses semantic topic clusters, not simple keyword matching

LinkedIn
Dwell time 為王 · 外連重罰 · 個人帳號才是主力
Dwell time is king · Links punished · Personal profiles rule
Week 4B2B 主場
▾
⚙️ 三個關鍵機制Three Key Mechanics
  • Dwell time 是首要品質指標：停留 61 秒+ 的貼文互動率 15.6% vs 0-3 秒的 1.2%（13 倍）——carousel/document 貼文 dwell time 是純文字的 2-3 倍
  • Dwell time is the top quality signal: 61s+ posts get 15.6% engagement vs 1.2% for 0-3s (13x) — carousels get 2-3x the dwell time of text
  • 外連重罰：帶外部連結觸及約 -60%；2026 起「連結放留言」繞法也被降權
  • External links punished: ~-60% reach; the "link in comments" workaround is also demoted since 2026
  • 個人 profile 佔 feed ~65%，company page 僅 ~5%——B2B 有機成長主力是員工個人帳號與高管思想領導
  • Personal profiles = ~65% of feed, company pages ~5% — B2B organic growth runs on personal accounts and executive thought leadership
  • 黃金 60 分鐘：發文後第一小時互動決定派發；頻率每週 3-5 則
  • Golden hour: first-hour engagement determines distribution; 3-5 posts/week
誤區：用公司頁當主力發文管道；貼文塞外連；engagement bait（「同意 +1」已被明確降權）。Pitfalls: company page as main channel; links in posts; engagement bait ("comment +1 if you agree" is explicitly demoted).

⚙️ 生態邏輯Ecosystem Logic
  • 70%+ 用戶行為從搜尋出發——內容要當 Google 結果寫，不當社群貼文寫（標題關鍵字、封面圖、清單式乾貨）
  • 70%+ of user behavior starts from search — write content like Google results, not social posts
  • 種草文化：真實使用心得取代硬銷；~80% 購買決策受種草筆記影響
  • Zhongcao culture: authentic reviews over hard selling; ~80% of purchase decisions influenced by seeding notes
  • 演算法偏好：真實性（廣告感重會限流）、利基垂直、互動速度
  • Algorithm favors: authenticity (ad-feel gets throttled), niche verticals, engagement velocity
  • 商業功能：品牌號、薯條（加熱）、蒲公英平台（KOL/KOC 撮合）
  • Commerce: brand accounts, paid boosting, Pugongying KOL/KOC marketplace
台灣法規：2025-12-04 起內政部對小紅書實施一年禁令（當時台灣用戶約 300 萬）。策略定位應為「華語市場（中國大陸、馬來西亞、港澳）渠道」而非台灣本地主力。使用前查證禁令現況。Taiwan ban: a one-year government ban started 2025-12-04 (~3M TW users at the time). Position RED as a Chinese-market channel (mainland China, Malaysia, HK/Macau), not a Taiwan-local one. Verify ban status before planning.
誤區：用 IG 思維經營（重人設輕搜尋）；筆記太像廣告被限流；只找頭部 KOL 忽略 KOC 素人矩陣（種草的信任基礎是「像我一樣的普通人」）。Pitfalls: IG-style persona-first operation; ad-like notes get throttled; chasing top KOLs while ignoring KOC networks (trust comes from "people like me").

Week 5電商導流 E-commerceMatt 主場
▾
⚙️ 為什麼手作賣家不能忽略它Why Makers Can't Ignore It
  • 97% 熱門搜尋是無品牌詞——最適合被「還不知道要買什麼牌子」的人發現
  • 97% of top searches are unbranded — ideal for discovery before brand choice
  • 購買意圖指數是社群平均 5.6 倍；平均 CPC $0.83 社群類最低；對購物網站 referral 比 Facebook 多 33%
  • Purchase intent 5.6x social average; $0.83 avg CPC (lowest); 33% more shopping referrals than Facebook
  • Pin 壽命以月/年計（IG 以小時計）——長尾流量資產，對 Etsy 賣家尤其被低估
  • Pins live for months/years (IG posts: hours) — an undervalued long-tail asset, especially for Etsy sellers
  • 受眾：女性 70.3%；核心類目＝家居、手作、食譜、穿搭、婚禮
  • Audience: 70.3% female; core = home, crafts, recipes, fashion, weddings
  • 頻率：每週 5-10 Pin 起步；高成效者每日 15-25，但 >50/日傷派發
  • Cadence: start 5-10 pins/week; top performers 15-25/day, but >50/day hurts
誤區：當社群追互動（該追搜尋曝光與外連點擊）；不做直式 2:3 + 文字覆蓋；board 命名不關鍵字化。工具：Pinterest Trends（免費關鍵字研究）。Pitfalls: chasing engagement like a social network (chase search impressions & outbound clicks); skipping 2:3 vertical + text overlay; non-keyworded board names. Tool: Pinterest Trends (free).

35+ 的第一平台 · 社團 = 平台內 owned audience
The #1 platform for 35+ · Groups = on-platform owned audience
Week 5熟齡受眾 Mature
▾
⚙️ 現況與廣告生態Reality & Ads
  • 品牌粉專有機觸及持續探底；演算法偏好 meaningful engagement 與朋友/社團內容
  • Page organic reach keeps falling; algorithm favors meaningful engagement and friends/Groups content
  • 社團（Groups）是最強有機工具：成員收到新貼文通知（粉絲沒有）＝平台內 owned audience
  • Groups are the strongest organic tool: members get notifications (page fans don't) = on-platform owned audience
  • 受眾：Gen X 與 Boomers 使用率皆 88%——觸及 35+ 的第一平台；FB Reels 受眾比 TikTok 老，適合 35-55
  • Gen X & Boomers both at 88% usage — the #1 platform for 35+; FB Reels skews older, good for 35-55
  • 廣告 2026：Advantage+（AI 自動投放）成預設；broad targeting + 強素材勝過細分受眾（素材即定向）；first-party data 是最可靠輸入
  • Ads 2026: Advantage+ is the default; broad targeting + strong creative beats micro-targeting (creative IS targeting); first-party data is the most reliable input
  • 熟齡投放注意：Meta 會把預算集中到 65+（便宜高轉換），要控年齡需手動設限
  • Note: Meta concentrates budget on 65+ (cheap conversions); set manual age caps if needed
誤區：宣稱「FB 已死」放棄 35+ 市場；只經營粉專不建社團；在 Advantage+ 時代仍花大量時間手動細分受眾而不迭代素材。Pitfalls: declaring "FB is dead" and abandoning 35+; running only a page without a Group; micro-managing audiences instead of iterating creative in the Advantage+ era.

實作練習 + 平台選擇框架
Practice + Platform Selection Framework
深耕 1-2 平台勝過全平台淺嚐
Going deep on 1-2 beats spreading thin
動手做 Hands-on
▾

平台選擇公式：受眾在哪 × 內容形式擅長什麼 × 能持續多久——三者交集才做。

Platform selection: where your audience is × what content you're good at × what you can sustain — only act on the intersection.

練習 1（W3）：三平台差異化發布 Exercise 1 (W3): Differentiated Publishing 做一支捕夢網生成過程 30 秒影片，寫出三平台各自的 caption（TikTok 口語關鍵字長 caption、Reels 100-300 字、Shorts 標題），實際發布並記錄一週數據。 Make one 30s process video; write platform-specific captions (TikTok conversational keywords, Reels 100-300 chars, Shorts title); publish and track for a week.
練習 2（W4）：同主題三種語氣 Exercise 2 (W4): One Topic, Three Voices 用同一主題寫三版：X 版（犀利短句）、Threads 版（對話式）、LinkedIn 版（carousel 大綱、正文無外連），體會平台語氣差異。 Write the same topic three ways: X (sharp), Threads (conversational), LinkedIn (carousel outline, no links in body).
練習 3（W5）：Pinterest 起步 + 平台備忘錄 Exercise 3 (W5): Pinterest Setup + Platform Memo ①用 Pinterest Trends 查捕夢網相關關鍵字，設計 5 個關鍵字化 board 名稱 + 10 個 Pin 標題。②寫一頁備忘錄：你的產品客群在哪三個平台交集最大？選出深耕的 1-2 個。 ① Use Pinterest Trends for dreamcatcher keywords; design 5 keyworded boards + 10 pin titles. ② Write a one-page memo: which 1-2 platforms will you go deep on, and why?
