最常見的錯誤是把三者當「新淘汰舊」。實際上 AIDA 管一頁文案的說服結構、AARRR 管全公司的量測體系、Flywheel 管顧客如何成為成長動力——三層同時存在。

The most common mistake is treating these as "new replaces old." In reality AIDA governs persuasion within a single page, AARRR governs company-wide measurement, and the Flywheel governs how customers power growth — all three layers coexist.

1️⃣ AIDA（1898, E. St. Elmo Lewis）
  • Awareness → Interest → Desire → Action，最古老的線性漏斗
  • 現代定位：轉換文案層面仍然有效——sales page、landing page、廣告腳本的段落結構
  • Modern role: still valid for conversion copy — sales pages, landing pages, ad scripts
  • 侷限：只走到成交為止，不處理留存與推薦；真實消費者會跳階、回流
  • Limit: stops at the sale; ignores retention & referral; real consumers skip and loop back
誤區：宣稱「AIDA 過時該淘汰」。錯的是拿它當整體成長模型，不是框架本身。Pitfall: declaring "AIDA is dead." What's wrong is using it as a growth model, not the framework itself.
2️⃣ AARRR / Pirate Metrics（2007, Dave McClure）
階段 Stage問題Question範例 KPIExample KPI
Acquisition 獲客使用者從哪來？Where do users come from?Sessions by source/mediumSessions by source
Activation 啟用第一次就體驗到價值？First-run value (aha moment)?完成關鍵動作率Key action completion
Retention 留存會回來嗎？Do they return?D7 / D30 retentionD7 / D30 retention
Revenue 營收願意付錢嗎？Do they pay?ARPU, conversion rateARPU
Referral 推薦會帶朋友來嗎？Do they refer?NPS, referral sessionsNPS
  • 核心價值：每階段對應可量測 KPI，讓團隊脫離 vanity metrics（虛榮指標）
  • Core value: every stage maps to a measurable KPI, escaping vanity metrics
  • 變體 RARRA：把 Retention 排最前——反映「留存比獲客重要」的現代共識
  • Variant RARRA: Retention first — reflecting the modern consensus that retention beats acquisition
誤區：一次優化全部五階段。正確做法是找出漏水最嚴重的一段（one metric that matters）集中火力。Pitfall: optimizing all five stages at once. Find the leakiest stage (one metric that matters) and focus.
3️⃣ Flywheel（2018, HubSpot）
  • Attract → Engage → Delight 循環，顧客位於圓心 loop, with the customer at the center
  • 核心論點：漏斗把顧客當「產出物」（掉出底部就結束）；飛輪把顧客當「動力來源」——口碑、評論、推薦帶動下一輪成長
  • Core thesis: funnels treat customers as output; the flywheel treats them as the force — word-of-mouth, reviews, referrals power the next cycle
  • 兩個操作變數：加「力」force（投資顧客成功）、減「摩擦」friction（移除購買與使用障礙）
  • Two levers: add force (invest in customer success), remove friction (eliminate obstacles)
  • 最適用：訂閱制、社群驅動、口碑效應強的產品
  • Best fit: subscriptions, community-driven, word-of-mouth products
誤區：以為飛輪取代漏斗所以不用管轉換率——飛輪內部每個階段仍然有轉換漏斗。Pitfall: assuming the flywheel replaces funnels — every flywheel stage still contains conversion funnels.

🤖 AI 行銷現況AI Marketing Today
  • HubSpot 2026 調查：86.4% 行銷人已用 AI；每週省 10-14 小時
  • HubSpot 2026: 86.4% of marketers use AI; saving 10-14 hrs/week
  • 關鍵矛盾：AI 內容量已超過人類但多為平均水準 → 「人味」成為差異化
  • Key paradox: AI content volume exceeds human output but is mostly average → "human touch" is now the differentiator
  • 贏家策略：human-led + AI-assisted——AI 省流程時間，人力集中在原創觀點與第一手數據
  • Winning strategy: human-led + AI-assisted — AI saves process time; humans focus on original perspectives and first-party data
🔍 AEO / GEO

AEO — Answer Engine Optimization

優化給 AI 搜尋功能（Google AI Overviews、featured snippets）直接引用你的內容。

Optimizing for AI search features (Google AI Overviews, featured snippets) to quote you directly.

GEO — Generative Engine Optimization

讓 LLM（ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini）生成回答時引用你為來源。

Getting LLMs (ChatGPT / Claude / Gemini) to cite you as a source in generated answers.

  • 公式：SEO + AEO + GEO——建立在 SEO 之上，不是取代
  • Formula: SEO + AEO + GEO — built on SEO, not replacing it
  • Princeton GEO 研究：加專家引言 +41% 可見度、統計數據 +30%、引用來源 +30%
  • Princeton GEO study: expert quotes +41% visibility, statistics +30%, citations +30%
  • 手法：question-first 結構、schema markup、原創數據、llms.txt
  • Tactics: question-first structure, schema markup, original data, llms.txt
誤區：砍 SEO 預算全轉 AEO——生成引擎依賴的權威訊號與傳統搜尋高度重疊。Pitfall: cutting SEO budget for AEO — generative engines rely on authority signals that heavily overlap with traditional search.
📱 社群平台搜尋化Social Search
  • 近 40% Gen Z 用 TikTok/IG 而非 Google 搜尋（Google 自家數據）；2026 年 49% 消費者曾把 TikTok 當搜尋引擎
  • Nearly 40% of Gen Z searches on TikTok/IG instead of Google (Google's own data); 49% of consumers used TikTok as a search engine in 2026
  • 搜尋內容偏好：教學影片 61% > 產品評測 45% > 個人故事 41%
  • Search content preference: tutorials 61% > reviews 45% > personal stories 41%
  • 影片口說文字、字幕、caption 關鍵字都被平台索引——社群內容要做「社群 SEO」
  • Spoken words, captions, and on-screen text are all indexed — social content needs "social SEO"
誤區：只在 hashtag 塞關鍵字——2026 索引主力是口說語音轉錄、螢幕文字與 caption，hashtag 權重已下降。Pitfall: keyword-stuffing hashtags — in 2026 the main index signals are speech transcripts, on-screen text, and captions.
🖱️ Zero-Click Content（Rand Fishkin / SparkToro）
  • 2026 年 Google 搜尋 68% 以零點擊結束（SparkToro 第一手研究）
  • 68% of Google searches end without a click in 2026 (SparkToro first-party research)
  • 策略轉向：從「優化網站流量」到「在受眾聚集處建立影響力」（LinkedIn、Reddit、YouTube、LLM），接受不可追蹤的多觸點
  • Strategic shift: from "optimizing site traffic" to "building influence where audiences gather," accepting untrackable touchpoints
  • 心法：zero clicks ≠ zero sales——品牌記憶在平台累積，購買時機到了自然回流
  • Mindset: zero clicks ≠ zero sales — brand memory accumulates on-platform and returns at purchase time
  • 指標改用：品牌搜尋量、直接流量、社群提及
  • New metrics: branded search volume, direct traffic, social mentions
💰 Creator Economy 現實檢查Reality Check
  • 2026 市場規模 $205B–$323B（各家統計口徑不同，取區間）；全球 2 億+ 創作者
  • 2026 market size $205B–$323B (varies by methodology); 200M+ creators globally
  • 收入殘酷長尾：半數年收 <$15K、僅 4% 超過 $100K、中位數約 $3K/年
  • Brutal long tail: half earn <$15K/year, only 4% exceed $100K, median ~$3K/year
  • 品牌贊助佔創作者收入約 70%；趨勢是經營 owned audience（email、會員制）脫離演算法依賴
  • Brand sponsorships = ~70% of creator income; the trend is building owned audiences (email, memberships) to escape algorithm dependence

🥤 Jobs-to-be-Done（Clayton Christensen）
  • 核心問句：「顧客雇用你的產品來完成什麼任務（job）？」
  • Core question: "What job does the customer hire your product to do?"
  • 奶昔案例：McDonald's 奶昔多在早晨售出——顧客雇用它「陪伴無聊通勤 + 單手可食 + 撐到中午」。競品不是其他甜點，是貝果、香蕉、無聊本身。做更濃稠後銷量成長
  • Milkshake case: morning milkshakes were hired to "accompany a boring commute + one-handed + last till noon." Competitors weren't desserts — they were bagels, bananas, and boredom itself
  • Job 三維度：functional（功能）、emotional（情緒）、social（社交）
  • Three job dimensions: functional, emotional, social
誤區：做成人口統計 persona——JTBD 刻意去人口統計化，同一個人在不同情境雇用不同產品。Pitfall: turning JTBD into demographic personas — JTBD is deliberately demographic-free; the same person hires different products in different situations.

  • 最重要的翻轉：顧客是英雄、品牌是嚮導（Yoda 之於 Luke）——多數品牌失敗因為把自己當英雄
  • The key inversion: the customer is the hero, the brand is the guide (Yoda to Luke) — most brands fail by casting themselves as the hero
  • 名言："If you confuse, you lose"——顧客買講得最清楚的，不是講得最聰明的
  • "If you confuse, you lose" — customers buy from whoever is clearest, not cleverest
  • 適用：官網首頁訊息、品牌敘事、elevator pitch、email 序列
  • Use for: homepage messaging, brand narrative, elevator pitch, email sequences
誤區：Problem 只寫外部問題——SB7 要求同時處理 internal（挫折感）與 philosophical（這不應該發生）層次，內部問題才是購買動機。Pitfall: only writing external problems — SB7 requires internal (frustration) and philosophical layers too; internal problems drive purchases.

  • 變動獎勵三類：Tribe（社交認同）、Hunt（資源獵取，如無限滾動）、Self（自我掌握感）
  • Three variable rewards: Tribe (social), Hunt (resources, e.g. infinite scroll), Self (mastery)
  • 行銷應用：內容系列化（養成回訪習慣）、email/推播設計、產品 onboarding
  • Marketing use: serialized content (habit-forming return visits), email/push design, onboarding
  • 附帶倫理：Eyal 本人提出 Manipulation Matrix 自我檢驗——負責任的習慣設計
  • Ethics: Eyal's own Manipulation Matrix for responsible habit design
誤區：獎勵做成固定（可預測的獎勵不形成習慣）；跳過 Investment 步驟（用戶無沉澱、隨時流失）。Pitfall: fixed rewards (predictable rewards don't form habits); skipping Investment (no user deposit, easy churn).

練習 1（W1）：AARRR 健檢 Exercise 1 (W1): AARRR Audit 用 AARRR 幫你的產品（如 telaaurealab.com）畫出五階段現況與 KPI：Acquisition = GA4 流量來源、Activation = 使用者第一次生成圖案、Revenue = 付款完成……找出漏水最嚴重的一段，寫下一個改善假設。 Map your product's five AARRR stages with KPIs (Acquisition = GA4 sources, Activation = first pattern generated, Revenue = checkout…). Identify the leakiest stage and write one improvement hypothesis.
練習 2（W2）：JTBD 陳述 Exercise 2 (W2): JTBD Statement 為你的產品寫一份 JTBD：誰、在什麼情境、雇用它完成什麼 job？functional / emotional / social 各是什麼？（例：手作愛好者雇用捕夢網生成器，在「想送獨一無二的禮物」情境下，完成「快速做出有個人意義的設計」——emotional job 是心意的表達。） Write a JTBD statement: who, in what situation, hires it for what job? What are the functional / emotional / social dimensions?
練習 3（W2）：SB7 重寫首頁 Exercise 3 (W2): SB7 Homepage Rewrite 用 StoryBrand 七元素重寫你網站首頁的 hero 區塊文案：顧客是英雄（不是你的產品）、指出 internal problem、你是給計畫的嚮導、一個清楚的 CTA。 Rewrite your homepage hero copy with SB7: customer as hero, name the internal problem, position yourself as the guide with a plan, one clear CTA.
